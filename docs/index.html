About

I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Bristol researching into segmentation and tracking in low-light videos, funded by MyWorld Strength in Places. I'm also a member of the Visual Information Laboratory and the Bristol Vision Institute, which are led by Prof. David Bull within the Department of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, University of Bristol. My supervisors are: Dr. Nantheera Anantrasirichai and Prof. David Bull.

Research Interests

  • Machine Perception
  • Image and video enhancement
  • Machine Learning
  • Computer Vision

Currently researching

  • Instance segmentation in low-light images
  • Segmentation and tracking in low-light videos
  • Tracking to improve low-light enhancement

News & Activities

Projects

Feature Denoising for Low-Light Instance Segmentation using Weighted Non-Local Blocks

This project tackled the challenge of instance segmentation in low-light images by using the proposed weighted non-local blocks to denoise the feature maps.
